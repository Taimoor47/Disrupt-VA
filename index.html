About Us

DisruptVA is a computer vision and video analytics solution provider that helps businesses harness the power of artificial intelligence (A.I) based computer vision technology to ramp up efficiency within the ethical perimeters that respect privacy and personal space of an individual or group. As a firm founded by machine & deep learning engineers, we at DisruptVA fully understand the implications of A.I. on society and the individual, so the ethical boundaries we set for ourselves lie at the heart of everything we do. A.I. is set to disrupt the ways things work. We believe the best way or perhaps the only way to adopt A.I. based technologies is to do so in human-first terms. The many transformations ... that computer vision is set to bring should all be prefaced by one thought before adoption: Is it for the overall good of the society and individual? The foundation of DisruptVA is grounded on this principle: espouse computer vision technology for the upliftment of business and society at large. That's how we define our mission statement. Based on this principle we choose NOT to offer facial recognition solutions - one of the most lucrative verticals in computer vision technology -because we believe that intrudes on the personal freedom of individuals.
