Optical Character Recognition

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is the blend of computer vision and machine learning technology that converts text or image from physical surfaces e.g., paper, into virtual or digital properties.

The technology enables the reading of any text or image and verifies its authenticity and helps store them digitally for use. Businesses use OCR to confirm serial tags and authenticate information for database maintenance, otherwise done manually.

About Us

Viday is a computer vision and video analytics bespoke solution provider that harnesses the power of artificial intelligence (A.I) to ramp up business efficiency across diverse industries and verticals. In simple words, we put business processes on steroid! But our business model differs markedly from others: we act within a strict self-imposed perimeter that respect privacy and personal space of an individual or group. As a firm founded by machine & deep learning engineers, we at Viday fully understand the implications of A.I. on society and the individual, so the ethical boundaries we set for ourselves lie at the heart of everything we do. A.I. is set to Viday the ways things work. We believe the best way or perhaps the only way to adopt A.I. based technologies is to do so in human-first terms. ... The many transformations that computer vision is set to bring should all be prefaced by one thought before adoption: Is it for the overall good of the society and individual? The foundation of Viday is grounded on this principle: espouse computer vision technology for the upliftment of business and society at large. That’s how we define our mission statement. Based on this principle we choose NOT to offer facial recognition solutions – one of the most lucrative verticals in computer vision technology -because we believe that intrudes on the personal freedom of individuals.

Viday is a computer vision and video analytics solution provider that helps businesses harness the power of artificial intelligence (A.I) based computer vision technology to ramp up efficiency within the ethical perimeters that respect privacy and personal space of an individual or group.
