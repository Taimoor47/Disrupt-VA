Optical Character Recognition..

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is the blend of computer vision and machine learning technology that converts text or image from physical surfaces e.g., paper, into virtual or digital properties.

The technology enables the reading of any text or image and verifies its authenticity and helps store them digitally for use. Businesses use OCR to confirm serial tags and authenticate information for database maintenance, otherwise done manually.

Converting soft documents into screeds of prints has been around for a while. But for a computer to accurately read text from a surface is something else. As the name suggests, Optical Character Recognition (OCR) identifies texts on any surface and reads them for a stated purpose.

By deploying machine learning techniques, we make the OCR process error-free. Our developer’s design software first clears the text of any blemishes and reduces the surface to the bare texts. And with the help of AI, the text is read pixel by pixel and matched against a database of fonts.

OCR can detect writing, no matter how complicated. Once trained, it’s an expert in recognizing text and can undertake complex tasks like counter-checking critical long digits identifications in a matter of seconds.
