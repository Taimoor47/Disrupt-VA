Data Analytics

The power of A.I. is unraveling fresh possibilities and taking us to unprecedented heights. A.I.-based video analytics employ the power of computer vision and trained the video cameras to be intelligent. From mere recorders of events, now AI can help videos identify and report elements within a given frame. Become active in surveillance and notify incidents in real-time.

This quantum leap in technology has changed the game for many industries and has knocked out the old ways with a power punch and disrupted structures within organizations.

The potential of video analytics is limitless. From manufacturing spaces to security and threat detection, video analytics not only lightens the burden of human efforts but does the job virtually error-free.

Video analytics can be used to secure a critical perimeter, count people in the crowd, detect behavior in a given context and the list goes on. Wherever the human eye is needed to perform a job, computer vision can take over and do it better.

The proliferation of portable cameras on smartphones and CCTV has made video data a fact of our every life. Now A.I. can help gather meaningful insights from such data and keep that proverbial eye open for good.
We train machines to read images and enable them to not only record but identify and interpret them to act in real-time on the information being gathered.

Viday is a computer vision and video analytics solution provider that helps businesses harness the power of artificial intelligence (A.I) based computer vision technology to ramp up efficiency within the ethical perimeters that respect privacy and personal space of an individual or group.
