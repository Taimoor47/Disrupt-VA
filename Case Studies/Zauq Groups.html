Brief overview of the client including the date and number of days spent on each project:

Company: Zauq Groups


Website: www.zauq.com

Time Spent on Project: ongoing
Zauq Group is a well-known company that engages in a variety of business activities such as trading, printing, packaging, industrial automation, research and development, and vertically growing technological divisions. Zauq Group has made a position for itself in the region by bringing in a number of world-leading products and technology. With an emphasis on research, we are able to offer bespoke solutions to fulfil market needs.

Challenge

The challenge faced was manually entering all the details of the receipts of the customers when any item is bought. Manually entering the details is a time taking job with many chances of errors. They needed a reliable and fast AI-Based Solution to overcome this challenge.

Viday's Solution

Zauq’s management decided to look for an AI solution provider who could make them a reliable and cost-effective product which can solve this issue.
We designed them a cost-effective product with following features:
  1. Capturing image and feeding the image to our AI based system.
  2. Pre Processing each receipt image
  3. Extracting all raw text
  4. Classifying Text according to the fields (Serial Number, Price, Quantity, etc)

Result

The device we created was a huge success, assisting Zauq’s management in overcoming this difficulty.

  1. We created a low-cost product.
  2. Accurate and reliable
  3. Time saving
  4. All results saved in an excel file by just clicking the export button
  5. An Interactive GUI to display and export results.
