A fully automated factory monitoring system that keeps an eye on 24-7

Company: Candel Thread


Website: www.candle-thread.com

Candle Threads is one of the world's largest makers of embroidery threads, producing over 100 million spools of thread every year, enough to cover the earth 6570 times.
They currently serve in 8 countries and are attempting to become the top global provider of Embroidery thread through our quality and services.

Challenge

Candle Thread is a massive organization with thousands of employees spread across a enormous factory. The key task was to monitor every nook and cranny of the plant simultaneously.
They have more than 200 CCTV cameras installed, but keeping eye on suspicious movement or activity through each camera is not only stressful but humanly impossible.
Human intervention increases the likelihood of overlooking critical events. The most important part of the monitoring was to watch out for any hint of fire or smoke. At such a large space looking out for smoke/fire is naturally difficult and normal smoke sensors aren’t smart and reliable too.

Viday's Solution

We installed an independent mechanism that automatically keep a watchful eye on the whole operation in the factory with minimum resource and time.
  1. A pipeline which loads and scans around 200 cameras within 15 seconds.
  2. On each camera, different AI models are working including person detection, vehicle detection and group detection.
  3. For each camera, total persons, total vehicles found is saved in the database along with the relevant image.
  4. If more than 4-5 people are gathered at a place, true alert is sent for group detection on that particular camera.
  5. Scanning each camera for smoke. If smoke is detected in any of the camera, the user is immediately sent an alert.

Result

The system we installed solved a huge problem for the business

  1. No Human intervention.
  2. Reliable system with very few chances of errors.
  3. 200 cameras scanned in just 15 seconds’ time.
  4. Results automatically saved in the local database.
  5. Easier for user to find desired activity and movement inside the factory with 100% results.
