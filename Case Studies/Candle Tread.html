100% accurate facial recognition system that made staff attendance a walk in the park!

Company: Candel Thread


Website: www.candle-thread.com

Time Spent on Project: 2-3months
Candle Threads is one of the world's largest makers of embroidery threads, producing over 100 million spools of thread every year, enough to cover the earth 6570 times.
They currently serve in 8 countries and are attempting to become the top global provider of Embroidery thread through our quality and services.

Challenge

Candle Threads is a massive corporation with thousands of employees. Recording each person's data for each day is a difficult task. They required a quick attendance system capable of precisely detecting and enrolling each person's face with their name. They were looking for a camera that was integrated with an AI-based solution that could solve their problem.

Viday's Solution

The system we installed was able to do following things:
  1. Option of enrolling around 1000 employees in a single camera.
  2. Recognizing each person’s face and displaying their name on the user interface.
  3. Saving each person’s time.
  4. Can detect person’s face on around 4-5 feet distance accurately.

Result

The system we installed proved out to be very beneficial for the team of candles.

  1. Almost 100% accurate results.
  2. Detecting and saving results of each person within milliseconds.
  3. Interactive User Interface showing person’s face and his name.
  4. Company’s database integrated.

Viday is a computer vision and video analytics solution provider that helps businesses harness the power of artificial intelligence (A.I) based computer vision technology to ramp up efficiency within the ethical perimeters that respect privacy and personal space of an individual or group.
