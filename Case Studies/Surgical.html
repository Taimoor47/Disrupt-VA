Injection Mold Inspection system with zero human intervention not only saved time but increased productivity

Company: Silver Surgical Complex


Website: www.sscltd.com.pk

Silver Surgical Complex Pvt Ltd has been a top medical equipment supplier to pharmaceutical industries for more than a decade now. Based out of Karachi Pakistan, they provide standard medical goods to essential medical department i.e., Anesthesia, Radiology, Emergency, Intensive Care Units, and Neonatal Segments in Hospitals.

Challenge

The injection molding process at Silver Surgical Complex was operated manually. A technician had to sit at the machine to determine if the molding process was working on the dot. If something goes wrong, he must stop the machine and fix it manually. The human involvement was slowing down the whole process and the quality was also not up to the mark.

Viday's Solution

They decided to automate the process to get rid of human intervention and ensure reliability with minimum resources. The only option was to integrate a camera with AI models. The solution we provided was:
  1. 2 cameras integrated together.
  2. Sensor placed at top of the machine, which identified the machine opening and closing.
  3. Sensor gave instructions to cameras, after which both the cameras take respective images which were sent to the AI models.
  4. If any problem is reported through AI model, user is alerted through an alarm and machine is automatically stopped at that instant.

Result

The artificial intelligence-based smart inspection system implemented at Silver Surgical Complex has been a big success.

  1. There will be no human interference at any point during the procedure.
  2. The system proved to be more cost-effective for them.
  3. There are less risks of mistake and more dependability.
